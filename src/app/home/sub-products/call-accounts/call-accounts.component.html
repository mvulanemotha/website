CALL ACCOUNTS

Status Capital Building Society is looking to introduce a Call Account which offers the advantages of both a Savings and a Cheque Account. A call-like cheque account, has no ﬁxed deposit period, provides instant access to funds, and allows unlimited withdrawals. This type of account also provides the investor with interest which is calculated daily but credited at the end of the month. This vehicle is perfect for large funds that may be called up anytime without the rigidity of a ﬁxed notice period and without having the value of the funds being depleted by charges should the funds be kept for a longer period. Our members and clients will be advised soon as we officially introduce the call account.

PERMANENT SHARES

These shares are not publicly marketed but can be negotiated with the building society on the request which will have to be approved by the board. They give the investor long-term membership in the building society and are suitable for high-net-worth individuals and institutional investors who have lump sums to save and want returns on their investments. The nature of permanent shares issued by Status Capital Building Society is such that these will provide the investors with equity in the building society. The building society, therefore, reserves the right to approve/decline any applicant and no transfer of permanent shares will be completed without the approval of the board of directors. These shares are not redeemable at the instance of the investor and they must be paid for in full before issue. The building society however reserves the right to redeem them at its own instance.

Customer service and customer satisfaction are very crucial to SCBS. This complaints procedure and our customer service approach is underpinned by the principle of treating customers fairly (TCF), of which there are six TCF outcomes which guide us and underpin our customer service.

Status Capital Building Society is committed to:

  • Resolving client complaints in a manner which SCBS believes is fair to our clients, our business and to our staff.
  • Ensuring that clients have full knowledge of the procedures established for internal resolution of their complaints, details of which will be given to them in writing.
  • Ensuring easy access to our complaintsâ€™ resolution facilities at any of our offices, or by way of post, telephone or email.
  • Employing and empowering properly trained people in our business to deal with complaints, as well as with the escalation of serious non-routine complaints.
  • Informing clients of their right to refer their complaints to the OFS should a complaint not be resolved to their satisfaction within six weeks from the date on which the complaint is received.

Complaints Procedure

Steps to follow:

  • Contact your branch.
  • Reduce the complaint to writing.
  • The branch will endeavour to resolve the complaint as soon as possible within a period not exceeding 30 days.
  • Where you are not satisfied with how the complaint is resolved or has not been resolved at all within such period please contact the Ombudsman of Financial Services.

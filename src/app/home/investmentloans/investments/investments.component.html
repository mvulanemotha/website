Fixed Deposit

Our monthly interest Fixed Deposit is offered for a minimum of two years and includes capital protection. Investors may opt to re-invest or withraw principal at maturity.

Fixed Period Shares

Fixed Period Shares are a medium-long term investment option that allows you to fix your capital for a period of 24, 36 or 60 months at a fixed interest rate.
This investment option protects you from the decrease of the interest over the fixed period and assures you the security of your capital.

Likusasa Fixed Deposit Account

Invest in your children's tomorrow by opening a Likusasa Fixed Account today. Our Likusasa Fixed Account is an investment account
tailor made specifically for minor children below the ages of 16 years.

It is the perfect option for parents (on behalf of thier children) to safeguard thier capital while earning or accumulating thier returns of interest.

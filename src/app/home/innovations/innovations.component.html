OVERVIEW OF THE SCBS TECHNOLOGY

Product & service offering

  1. The Status Capital Building Society Mobile App and Website originates loans and allows for customers to complete transactions instantly and at any time or location.
  2. Status Capital Building Society offers a range of products across the banking, credit and insurance sector. Depending on the product selected by the customer, their application process will be channelled appropriately.
  3. Status Capital Building Society offers a range of AI systems, to help with fraud prevention, credit scoring, automated income and expenses calculator, conversions engine, client churn model, collections algorithm and a loan uptake predictor.
  4. Status Capital Building Society developed its own proprietary loan management system, AL Bank which enables SCBS to manage the full loan cycle.
  5. Customer transaction records are automatically pushed to the Accounting Ledger from the AL Bank system. Once in the ledger, financial reports can be generated to ensure that the accounting position of the data is stored in an SQL Database hosted in our secure data centre. We use real-time replication to keep our data warehouse and reports up to date.

OUR BUSINESS MODEL

As a building society, we are a mutual organisation and are proudly owned by our members. Being owned by our members means that Status Capital Building Society consequently offers better interest rates on savings and lower mortgage rates. At the heart of our business is to satisfy common needs. Although our focus is more on financial products like savings and mortgages, we are still able to tailor-make and offer the same services that banks provide.
